# ⏱️ Time Tracker

A modern productivity timer that helps you focus, take breaks, and track your work sessions. Perfect for Pomodoro technique, time blocking, or any productivity method.

![Time Tracker Screenshot](https://via.placeholder.com/800x450/2563eb/ffffff?text=Time+Tracker+App)

## ✨ Why Time Tracker?

**🎯 Focus Better**  
Clean, distraction-free interface keeps you focused on what matters. Large timer display and smooth progress animation help you stay in the zone.

**⚡ Never Lose Progress**  
Your timer keeps running even if you close the browser or restart your computer. Session data is safely stored locally and available offline.

**📊 Understand Your Patterns**  
Track time across different activities and see how you actually spend your day. Build better work habits with data-driven insights.

**🎨 Feels Great to Use**  
Beautiful dark/light themes, customizable notifications, and thoughtful interactions make time tracking enjoyable rather than a chore.

## 💡 How to Use

### Basic Workflow

1. **Set your timer** - Click the time to customize duration
2. **Pick an activity** - Categorize what you're working on
3. **Start focusing** - Hit play and get to work
4. **Take breaks** - Use the break button when sessions complete
5. **Review progress** - Check your productivity in Activities

### Pro Tips

- **Break mode**: Switch to break timer after work sessions
- **Archive activities**: Keep your list clean by archiving old projects
- **Custom sounds**: Upload your own notification sounds in Settings
- **Keyboard shortcuts**: Spacebar to start/pause, ESC to reset
- **Multiple devices**: Your data syncs across all your browsers
